Post-IOL Excursion

There are limited seats of 90 people for each plan, first come first go.

Send us the names of participants who will join the excursion and we will invoice you.

(1) Seoul & DMZ (max. 90 people) - 330 USD/person, hotel included

Visit the soul of the country, the capital Seoul Metropolitan City, and the Demilitarized Zone that borders North Korea. 

Day 1 (Aug 3) - Old town of Seoul

- Gyeongbokgung Palace: arguably the most beautiful and the largest of all five palaces in Korea

- Bukchon Hanok village: home to hundreds of traditional houses (hanok), which date back to the Joseon Dynasty.

- Insa-dong: full of galleries and traditional restaurants, teahouses, and cafes, that you can experience traditional Korean artworks and handcrafts

- Nanta show: non-verbal comedy show which incorporates traditional samul nori rhythm

- Check-in at a hotel in Seoul

Day 2 (Aug 4) - DMZ

- DMZ: a strip of land running across the Korean Peninsula that serves as a buffer zone between North Korea and South Korea.

- Han river cruise and Seoul N tower: visit the two greatest landmarks of Seoul! 480m above sea level, making it one of the tallest towers in Asia.

Day 3 (Aug 5) - Seoul

- Oil Tank Culture Park: historically significant site while raising awareness of sustainability and urban regeneration

- MBC World Broadcasting Theme Park: various Hallyu ('Korean wave') contents all in one place

- Itaewon Gyeongridan-gil: cosmopolitan dining and nightlife, with Korean BBQ restaurants, upscale bistros, as well as low-key kebab shops catering to a late-night crowd

Departure (Aug 6)

- Check-out hotel and move to Incheon Airport

More at: https://www.visitseoul.net/index

https://www.highheelsandabackpack.com/visiting-the-dmz-korea-part-one/

(2) Jeju Island (max. 90 people) - 550 USD/person, hotels & flights inlcuded

For South Korea, the top island destination is undoubtedly the picturesque Jeju Island. Visit Jeju and feel the summer breeze of Korea.

Day 1 (Aug 3) - Gimpo to Jeju

- Pick-up at 8am, flight from Gimpo Airport to Jeju (11:20 ~ 12:30)

- Aewol Coastal Road: the most famous of all coastal roads in Jeju.

- Hallim Park: palm trees in 330,578㎡ of wilderness, haven of green

- Check-in at a hotel in Jeju

Day 2 (Aug 4) - Jeju

- Ecoland Theme Park: gives you a great chance to explore the primitive forest of Mt. Halla "Gotjawal"

- Woljeongri Beach: picturesque scenery and beautiful white sand

Day 3 (Aug 5) - Jeju to Seoul

- Mt Halla the 1100 Altitude Wetland: rich wildlife, including various endangered and indigenous species, beautiful landscapes, and geographical features. The wetland has been designated not only as one of the national Wetland Protected Areas.

- Drop at Jeju airport at 14:30, flight from Jeju to Gimpo

- Check-in at a hotel in Seoul

Departure (Aug 6)

- Check-out hotel and move to Incheon Airport

More at: https://www.visitjeju.net
